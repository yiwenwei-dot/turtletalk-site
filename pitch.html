The Solution

A tiny sea turtle. A giant daily ritual.

Meet Tammy — a voice companion who listens to kids and gently bridges them back to their family.

01
Talk
Child taps the turtle and has a warm, voice-first conversation about their day, worries, or wins.
02
Reflect
Tammy gently reframes hard moments and sends the child home with one small 'brave mission' for the week.
03
Grow
Parents get a dinner-time prompt ('Ask about Mia today') — turning a solo moment into a family one.
